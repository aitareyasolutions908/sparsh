Brigade Metropolis is a mixed-use development in Whitefield, Bangalore. It consists of 1500+ residential apartments, a retail block, and commercial office spaces. The property has blue-chip tenants including E&Y, Honeywell, GE, and EYGBS India. It was developed by the Brigade Group, one of the leading property developers in South India.
The Commercial part of the development is Tower A and Tower B which consists of over 7.5 lakhs sft of Grade A Commercial Property. The Investment opportunity is the third floor in Tower A (22540 sft) occupied by Capgemini, a Fortune 200 company. The per square foot cost of the property is approximately INR 8607/sft with a rental yield of 8.76% and an IRR of 18%.

The property is located on the Whitefield main road, in one of the fastest growing residential and IT hubs in Bangalore. The property is situated close to an upcoming metro station which is expected to open in 2021. It also has extensive city bus connectivity.

Each tower spans 13 floors (2B +G+12) and measure 2,86,000 sqft. Strata's investment opportunity is located on the 3rd Floor of Tower A measuring about 22,540 sft of office space and is completely leased to Capgemini.
