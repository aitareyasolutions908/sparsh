How it
Works

Sparsh infra provides a seamless, transparent, and lucrative way to make investments in Commercial Real Estate. Here's how we do it.

Choose Property

Choose properties that we have shortlisted after conducting rigorous due diligence, market review, rental yields, target returns and legal title.

Buy Your Share

When a property is listed, investors can review the offer in-depth before paying a 10% token advance to book their fraction.

Once the property is fully-funded, investors are made shareholders in a Special Purpose Vehicle (SPV) through which they will own the property. Sprash Infra manages the asset on the investors’ behalf.

Receive Distributions

Start receiving distributions on your investmentstraight to your bank account.

  • Monitor & Sell Investments
  • Everything related to the investment, from its current value to lease updates and total earnings can be tracked on our online dashboard
  • List your shares for potential sale at any time(no lock-in) or wait for the entire property to be sold
